# Authorship record - CoffeeNutz Guide

**Author and rights holder:** Ömer Aydın (CoffeeNutz). Sole author and director.

This file documents the human authorship behind CoffeeNutz Guide, so that creative
control and ownership are clear and on record. Copyright in Türkiye is automatic
from creation (Law No. 5846); this record exists to make the human authorship
provable in a dispute and registrable where registration requires it.

## Human authorship

CoffeeNutz Guide was conceived, designed, and directed by Ömer Aydın, drawing on
15 years as a coffee roaster, 11 years teaching to SCA format, SCA AST and Q Grader
Instructor credentials, and the ZHAW CAS Coffee Excellence program. The following
creative elements are his original authorship and selection:

- The product concept: one modular app spanning Roast, Cup, Grade, and Value
  Assessment on a shared data layer, and the roast to cup to grade link.
- The roast guide engine: the cinnamon-and-first-crack calibrate-then-guide model,
  the stage map, the pacing and learning logic, and the recipe heuristics.
- The Value Assessment vision: Fit versus Distinctiveness as two separate readouts,
  the identity card, and the position that the distinctive attribute - not the
  score - is what makes a coffee worth more.
- The Distinctiveness method: deriving distinctiveness from specificity, profile
  peakedness, standout sections, and rarity against the user's own cupping library,
  while keeping the one-line human attribute as the apex judgement.
- The selection, arrangement, wording, and interface decisions throughout.

## Tools and assistance

Software development tools, including AI coding assistants, were used as
instruments under Ömer Aydın's direction. All design decisions, the assessment
logic, the curation of what the app does, and the creative arrangement are his.
AI was used to implement his instructions, not to originate the work.

## Maintaining the record

- Keep the source repository private; its commit history timestamps authorship.
- Log major design decisions here with dates as the app evolves.
- If anyone else ever contributes code, obtain a written assignment of rights
  before merging.
